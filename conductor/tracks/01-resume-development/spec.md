# Spec: Resume Development Assessment

## Objective
Assess the current state of the BudgetFlow MVP and determine the exact next steps required to complete the initial functional scope, taking into account the legacy `plan-stageX.md` progress.

## Scope
- Review existing codebase (UI, ViewModels, Repositories).
- Cross-reference with the MVP requirements defined in `conductor/product.md`.
- Identify missing features, pending bugs, or UI polish needed to finalize the MVP.

## Output
The result of this track will be a comprehensive `plan.md` outlining the actionable steps to complete the MVP.

## Acceptance Criteria
- A clear list of pending MVP tasks is documented.
- The next actionable track (e.g., `02-implement-dashboard`, etc.) is identified and ready to be specced out.
